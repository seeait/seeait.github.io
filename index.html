Motivation & Goals

Generative AI (GenAI) is reshaping software-intensive organizations. SEE-AIT aims to clarify the role of software engineering in organizational GenAI transformation and establish principles, practices, and open research questions :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.

Call for Papers

We invite extended abstracts (up to 5 pages) and full papers (5–8 pages) addressing challenges and solutions related to organizational GenAI transformation :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}.

Topics of Interest & Submission Guidelines

  • LLM and agentic GenAI integration in organizational workflows
  • Change management for GenAI adoption
  • Software architecture for GenAI-enabled systems
  • Metrics for transformation and system reliability
  • Tools and methods for human–AI collaboration

Important Dates (AoE)

  • Paper Submission: 12 February 2026
  • Notification: 19 March 2026
  • Camera-ready: 2 April 2026
